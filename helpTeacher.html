Frequently Asked Questions

Administrative guidelines and solutions.

How do I assign a new project to a class?
Go to the "Projects" module and click "Create New". You must select the specific Class (e.g., Grade 10-A) and Subject (e.g., Physics). Set the deadline and attach guidelines before publishing.
Where can I view and grade student submissions?
Click on the specific Project card to open the dashboard. Navigate to the "Submissions" tab. Here you can view uploaded files, verify timestamp (On Time/Late), and enter marks/remarks directly.
How can I edit a published test schedule?
In the "Examination" module, find the test in the "Upcoming" list. Click the "Edit" icon. Note that you cannot change the schedule if the test start time is within 1 hour.
How do I download monthly attendance reports?
Navigate to "Analytics" > "Class Reports". Select the Class and Month. Click the "Export as PDF" or "Export as CSV" button at the top right corner.
